How to Play

▼
  • Goal: Guide both colored balls to their matching colored targets.
  • Movement: When you move in any direction:
    • Red ball moves in the direction you choose
    • Blue ball moves in the opposite direction
  • Controls:
    • Use arrow keys on desktop
    • Use on-screen arrows on mobile
  • Reset: You can reset positions at any time (+5 moves penalty)
  • Win: Get both balls to their targets in the fewest moves possible!
